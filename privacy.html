Overview

Easy Fit helps you track food and workouts. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What We Collect

  • Account data: Email, display name, auth identifiers (via Firebase Auth).
  • App data you provide: Food logs, workout logs, preferences, measurements you enter.
  • Device & diagnostics: App/version, device model, OS, crash/analytics events.
  • Purchases: Subscription status and receipts/transaction IDs (Apple In-App Purchase).
  • Advertising identifiers: If ads are enabled, AdMob/mediated networks may access device advertising IDs subject to your platform settings.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve app features (logs, goals, insights).
  • Authenticate, prevent abuse, and secure the service.
  • Process subscriptions and manage “No Ads”.
  • Show or remove ads depending on your purchase status.
  • Customer support and required communications.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted providers to run Easy Fit. Their use of data is governed by their policies:

  • Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, etc.).
  • Apple In-App Purchases (subscriptions and receipts).
  • Google AdMob & mediation (ads, when enabled).
  • FatSecret Platform API (nutrition database).
  • Zyla Labs API (calorie calculations for activities).

Ads & Tracking

If you haven’t purchased “No Ads”, ad partners may collect device identifiers and approximate location to show/measure ads. You can limit tracking in iOS Settings. Purchasing “No Ads” disables our banner and interstitial ads in the app.

Data Retention

We retain your account and app data while your account is active. You may request deletion; we’ll delete or de-identify data we’re not required to keep.

Sharing

We don’t sell your personal data. We share data with processors (the services above) to operate the app, and when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Children

Easy Fit is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
